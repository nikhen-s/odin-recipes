Gochujang Stir-Fried Chicken

Description: Chicken that is stir fried, with a gochujang sauce

A simple and easy dish to make that is really delicious.

Sauce Ingredients

  • 2.5 tablespoons of gochujang
  • water to taste
  • sesame oil
  • honey (1.25 tablespoons)
  • 0.3 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce

Fresh Ingredients

  • scallions
  • salt
  • garlic
  • shallots/onions (5 shallots or 1.5 onion)
  • chicken thighs
  • cooking oil
  • sesame seeds

What to do baddie:

  1. First, create your sauce with the sauce ingredients.
  2. Heat up some cooking oil under medium heat.
  3. Add your chicken thighs, cook until both sides brown and inside is fully cooked.
  4. Add scallions and shallots
  5. Add sauce
  6. Add sesame seeds
  7. Serve as you shouldd
